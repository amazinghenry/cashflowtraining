About Cash Flow Training 2022

This training is designed to help business owners understand business cash-flow reports (and cash-flow) at the most fundamental level.

The goal is to provide business owners with tools and insights to fully understand cash and also, introduce methodologies and simple structures that would help them achieve their cash flow objectives.

Objectives

Get entrepreneurs to have a 360 understanding of how cash flows in and out of their business.

Learn to plan and manage their cash-flow.

Learn to grow their business and sustain liquidity.
